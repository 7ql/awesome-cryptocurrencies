- GridCoin (Proof of Work)
- VeriBlock (Proof of Proof)

- Zcoin
- G1 (Web of trust)
- PI (Proof of User/Weak web of trust)
- Perlin (Avalanche DAG)
- IOST (Proof of Believability)
- Burst (Proof of Capacity)
